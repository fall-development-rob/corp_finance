# Trade Policy Analysis

Trade policy and energy supply analysis using EIA, WTO, and USASpending data from `geopolitical-trade`.

## What It Does
Analyses trade policy exposure: tariff rates, trade barriers, bilateral trade flows, energy supply data, and federal spending patterns. Outputs a trade risk assessment with sector impact analysis.

## Agent
Routes to `cfa-macro-analyst`.

## Key Tools
`wto_tariffs`, `wto_barriers`, `wto_trade_stats`, `eia_petroleum`, `usaspending_contracts`

## Usage
Specify a country pair (e.g., "US-China tariffs"), a sector (e.g., "semiconductors trade barriers"), or an energy query (e.g., "US crude oil inventory").
